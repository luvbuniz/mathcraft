═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Bright cartoony voxel-cube animation — soft rounded cube blocks, bright flat
colors, sunny and warm, smooth toy-like motion. 16:9, 720p. No text on screen,
no subtitles. Clear friendly young kids' voices, cheerful music, fun sound
effects. Original cube characters — NOT Minecraft Steve, NOT Minecraft skins.

CHARACTER LOOKS (keep identical every shot):
- LEO: a happy voxel boy, messy brown hair, yellow t-shirt, blue shorts.
- FIONA: a clever voxel girl, brown hair, purple dress.
- MOM: a warm, smiling voxel mom.
- SPARK: a small glowing fairy with sparkly wings, kind and gentle.
- ICE CREAM MAN: a towering, greasy-grinned man, cold stare, too-wide smile.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
## SEGMENT 1 — The five dollars
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
A sunny blocky neighborhood. MOM smiles and hands a crisp $5 bill to LEO and
FIONA on the porch. They light up and race down the sidewalk toward a jingling
ice cream truck.
- MOM: "Five dollars — one cone each, and bring me back the change, okay?"
- LEO & FIONA: "Thank you, Mom!"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
## SEGMENT 2 — The gross cheat
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
At the ice cream truck looms the ICE CREAM MAN — towering, greasy grin, cold
stare. A little sign reads "Cones $3.50." He slaps down two small cones. Leo
proudly hands over the whole $5 bill. The man makes a big show of counting
"change," then drops just two small coins (fifty cents) into Leo's palm and
SLAMS the window shut, cackling. The kids look confused, unsure if that's right.
- ICE CREAM MAN (booming, gleeful): "There's your change, ha-HAA! Now SCRAM!"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
## SEGMENT 3 — Fiona guesses, Leo runs
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
The kids stare at the two little coins, unsure. Fiona turns them over, guessing.
The man's laughter booms. Leo's cheeks burn with embarrassment; he turns and
bolts away — and trips into a glowing swirling PORTAL hole that opens in the
sidewalk, vanishing with a yelp. Fiona leaps in after him.
- FIONA (baffled): "Wait… fifty cents? Is that right? What's going on?"
- LEO (embarrassed): "I don't know either! Are we dumb?"
- FIONA (gasping): "LEO!"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
## SEGMENT 4 — Stackadoo World
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Leo and Fiona land softly in a dazzling voxel wonderland — rainbow hills,
candy-bright blocks, floating islands. Leo sits up, upset and lost. A gentle
sparkle: SPARK the fairy flutters down, warm and kind.
- LEO: "Where ARE we? I just wanted ice cream…"
- SPARK: "Welcome to Stackadoo World! Oh, you poor things — you look so muddled.
  Come, tell me what happened."

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
## SEGMENT 5 — The lesson (counting up)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SPARK waves her wand; glowing coins float over a number path. She counts UP from
the price to the five dollars. As Leo understands, his eyes go clear and bright.
- SPARK: "Your cones cost three dollars fifty. You paid five. Let's count UP!
  Start at $3.50… add fifty cents, that's $4.00… add a dollar, that's $5.00!"
- SPARK: "How much did we add back? Fifty cents and a dollar — that's one dollar
  and fifty cents!"
- LEO (lighting up): "One dollar fifty! But that crook only gave me FIFTY CENTS —
  he kept a whole dollar!"
- SPARK: "Now you see. A kid who can count can't be cheated."

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
## SEGMENT 6 — The Stackadoo coin
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SPARK presses a glowing gold coin into each of their palms. The coins flash and
the kids are gently swept back through the portal.
- SPARK: "Take a Stackadoo coin. Whenever you're stuck, hold it to your heart and
  say… 'Stackadoo!' — and I'll help you learn."
- LEO & FIONA: "Stackadoo!"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
## SEGMENT 7 — The catch
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Back at the ice cream truck. Leo counts the change in his palm, clear-eyed and
confident, as a crowd of other kids gathers. He calls out the cheat; the other
kids realize they were shorted too. A SHERIFF strolls up and hauls the
sputtering Ice Cream Man away.
- LEO (bold): "Hey! Two cones are $3.50. I paid $5. My change is $1.50 — and you
  only gave me FIFTY CENTS! You owe me a whole dollar more!"
- A KID IN THE CROWD: "He shorted me too!"  ANOTHER KID: "And me!"
- ICE CREAM MAN (sputtering): "I— uh— honest mistake!"  SHERIFF: "Sure it was."

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

- NEW OWNER: "Honest scoops, and the change counted right — every time!"
- LEO: "Smart kids don't get tricked."
- FIONA: "Stackadoo!"
