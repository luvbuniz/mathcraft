# 🎬 Stackadoo — Play Store Trailer Brief (~30s, 16:9)

**Goal:** convert store-listing visitors. **Rule #1:** show the REAL game — use AI
only for polished logo bumpers, not to replace gameplay (Play can reject promos
that misrepresent the app, and parents dislike bait-and-switch).

**Format:** 16:9, 1080p, ~30s. Upload to YouTube (unlisted ok) → paste the link
into the store listing's "YouTube video" field (it takes a link, not a file).

## Storyboard
| Time | Visual | On-screen text | Source |
|------|--------|----------------|--------|
| 0–3s | Hero chibi kid holds up a glowing block, sparkles | **Stackadoo** · "Build worlds. Solve math." | 🤖 AI bumper |
| 3–10s | Solve 6×3=? → tap 18 → confetti → block flies in → place it → house grows | "Solve math → earn blocks → BUILD" | 🎮 Real gameplay |
| 10–15s | Drive a voxel car past shops; pan a furnished house + pet | "Build, drive, explore a whole world" | 🎮 Real gameplay |
| 15–21s | Fast cuts: Reading Town · spelling bee · a Library cartoon · a badge | "Math · Reading · Spelling · Grammar" | 🎮 Real gameplay |
| 21–26s | Calm beat, kid waves | "No ads. No chat. Made by parents." | 🎮 + text |
| 26–30s | Logo bounce-in | "Stackadoo — free on Google Play" | 🤖 AI bumper |

## Voiceover (optional, warm narrator)
"Meet Stackadoo — where kids earn blocks by solving real math, then build a whole
world. Math, reading, spelling, and grammar, all wrapped in play. No ads, no chat —
just learning that feels like building. Stackadoo."

## AI bumper prompts
**Intro (0–3s):**
> Bright cartoony voxel scene, soft rounded cubes, sunny green meadow. A cute chibi
> kid (big rounded head, brown hair, purple dress) holds up a single glowing rainbow
> building block, beaming, as colorful cubes and sparkles float up. Slow push-in.
> Warm polished 3D render, cheerful, 16:9.

**Outro (26–30s):**
> Clean friendly end card: the Stackadoo logo — three soft rounded voxel cubes
> stacked (orange, gold, green) with a sparkle — centered on a soft sky-blue
> background with blocky clouds, gentle bounce-in. The word "Stackadoo" in chunky 3D
> block letters below. 16:9.

## Capturing gameplay (free)
- Tablet built-in screen recorder, or OBS on desktop at stackadoo.com/play.html.
- Capture: a math solve + block place, a car drive, the spelling bee, a Library
  cartoon, a badge earned.
- Edit in CapCut / iMovie with upbeat music + the on-screen text above.

## Notes
- Keep the first 3 seconds irresistible (the hook decides whether they watch on).
- Show the learn→build loop EARLY — it's the differentiator.
- Capture gameplay AFTER the avatar glow-up so the characters look their cutest.
