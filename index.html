✏️ Parent tip: keep a pencil and paper next to the tablet. For the bigger problems, encourage your child to write it down and work it out — stacking the numbers, jotting the tens and ones, or drawing it — instead of doing it all in their head. Working it out on paper builds real number sense (and makes the tricky ones much easier!).
