Questions

Good to know

Do I need to download anything?

No. Stackadoo runs right in your browser. On a tablet or phone you can “Add to Home Screen” for an app-like icon that even works offline.

Is it really safe for kids?

Yes. There’s no advertising, no chat, no contact with strangers, and no purchasable in-game currency. The only purchase is one optional unlock, made by a parent.

What ages is it for?

It’s tuned for grades 2–5 (roughly ages 7–11), with difficulty that adapts up and down to fit your child.

What does the $4.99 include?

It makes you a Founding Family: you get the early/beta version other parents don’t have yet, the paid worlds (Reading Town now; Science Park coming soon, included), and every world and update we add later — free, for life. One payment, no subscription, never again.

Can my kids play on more than one device?

Yes. Tap ☁️ Sign in on the home screen (Google or email) and your purchase and every child’s worlds follow you to any device automatically — buy once, sign in anywhere. (A grown-up signs in; kids stay nicknames — no child data is collected.)

I paid but a world is still locked on another device — help!

Tap ☁️ Sign in on that device with the same account and it’ll unlock automatically. Still stuck? Email us and we’ll sort it right away: support@stackadoo.com.
