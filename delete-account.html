Delete your Stackadoo account

Stackadoo is made by Buni LLC. This page explains how to delete your account and any data synced to it.

How to request deletion

  1. Email us from the email address you used to sign in:
    support@stackadoo.com
  2. Use the subject line “Delete my Stackadoo account.”
  3. We’ll confirm it’s you and delete your account and synced data within 30 days — usually much sooner.

You can also sign out at any time inside the game (tap ☁️ → Sign out), which unlinks your account from that device.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and the email address used to sign in.
  • Any worlds and progress synced to the cloud under that account.

What is kept

  • Local game saves stay on your own device until you uninstall Stackadoo or clear the app’s data — these live only on the device and are never sent to us.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties, and there is no retention beyond the 30-day deletion window above.
🔒 Stackadoo collects no data from children. Sign-in is an optional, grown-up feature used only to sync a family’s saves across devices — and you can remove it any time with the steps above.
