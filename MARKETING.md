## What it is
**Stackadoo** (stackadoo.com) — a free 3D voxel game where kids do **math, reading,
spelling, and grammar** to earn coins, then **build, drive, and decorate** their own
worlds. Runs in any browser, installable, works offline. No ads, no chat, kid-safe.

## The founder story (the hook)
**A mom built a 3D math game her 7-year-old twins beg to play.** True, and it's
the strongest angle — moms are most of the buyers. Twins: Fiona (reader/decorator/
homebody) and Leo (explorer/driver). The game grew from watching what each twin
actually loves.

## Who buys (and where they are)
- **Buyers = moms.** Channels: **Facebook** (mom/parenting/homeschool groups),
  **Instagram** (Reels), **TikTok** (short gameplay clips). These drive sales.
- **X (Twitter)** = the *founder/build-in-public* story — credibility & networking,
  not direct sales.

## The two big hooks
1. **"My twins beg to play the math game I built them."** (emotional, shareable)
2. **Parent payoff:** the grown-ups dashboard shows a secret grade-equivalency —
   *"your 3rd grader is doing 4th-grade-level math."* Aspirational, screenshot-worthy.
   (Framed as growth, never "behind.")

## The offer (free → paid)
- **Math Meadow is free forever.** Lead with this — it's polished and the math is
  verified correct at every level.
- **$4.99 one-time "Founding Family" unlock:** early/beta access other parents don't
  have yet + **Reading Town** + **every future world & update, free for life.** No
  subscription. Makes buying *now* the smart move.

## Trust signals to mention
Kid-safe: **no ads, no chat, no data collection** (COPPA-friendly, all local),
made by a mom, 30-day no-questions refund.

## 🌟 What's in the game now (the marketable surface)

**Math Meadow (free):** adaptive math (grades 2–5), the build-a-house sandbox,
vehicles, pets, photos/selfies, level-ups with bonus mini-games.

**Reading Town (the paid unlock)** — three learning "hubs" you walk into:
- **📚 Library** — read a fun story, spot the simile/metaphor, earn coins.
- **🏤 Post Office** — a real spoken **Spelling Bee** that gets harder as you improve.
- **🎬 Grammar Cinema** — *new!* fix the grammar in a "movie script," then it
  **premieres** with a marquee + confetti. (Capitalization, commas, tense, etc.)
- **🏡 Stack Realty** — buy a move-in home (cozy ranch → **furnished Grand Mansion**).
- **🚒 City vehicles** — *new!* fire truck & ambulance (**sirens + flashing lights**),
  taxi, city bus — plus the police car, monster truck, sports cars.

**Crowd-pleasers across worlds:**
- **🏊 Swimming pools** (3 sizes) incl. the **Mega Pool with a water slide** you ride
  **in first-person**, then splash. *(new)*
- **🔤 Level-up bonus games** — earn a mini-game when you level up: treasure-map (X,Y),
  describing-words, and **Unscramble**. *(new)*
- **↩️ One-tap Undo**, **auto-save**, and a **🛟 Back-up** button (kid progress is safe).

## 🎬 Content shot list

**Capture on a real tablet. Vertical (9:16) for TikTok/Reels; grab a few horizontal
for X/FB. Real kid hands + good light = authentic and converts.**

### Video clips (15–30s, the workhorses)
1. **The loop:** solve a math problem → coins/blocks burst → place a block/build.
   Caption: *"She does math to earn blocks. She has no idea she's learning."*
2. **Driving montage (likely your best performer):** the **fire truck & ambulance**
   (siren + flashing lights), the **police car**, the **monster truck** bouncing —
   drive through town. Caption: *"Built my son a math game. He just wants to drive the
   fire truck."*
3. **Water slide (huge):** climb the **Mega Pool** slide → ride down in **first-person**
   → splash. Caption: *"He did 10 math problems to afford the water slide."*
4. **Grammar Cinema premiere:** fix the script → *"NOW SHOWING"* marquee + confetti.
   Caption: *"A grammar game my kid actually asks to play."*
5. **Level up → bonus mini-game:** answer right → "LEVEL UP!" → the **Unscramble** or
   **treasure-map (X,Y)** game pops up. Shows real learning + reward.
6. **Spelling Bee:** the word is spoken aloud → kid spells it → it ramps up. Shows the
   "real teaching" depth.
7. **Buy & decorate a home:** Stack Realty → a move-in house appears → furnish it
   (the **furnished mansion** is a wow).
8. **Parent reveal:** the dashboard *"working at ≈ Grade 4 math / ahead of grade!"*
   Caption: *"The part parents love."*
9. **Selfie feature:** strike a pose with the avatar, snap a photo.
10. **Raw 60 seconds** of your kid actually playing — unpolished, build-in-public.
