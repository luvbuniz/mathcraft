How to play
Arrows/WASD move Space jump E use / sit D build R remove I backpack L lie down F throw

Two ways to play with a mouse (pick whichever you like):

🚶 Click-to-move (no keyboard needed): turn on the 🚶 Click-to-move button (bottom-left), then left-click the ground to walk there. Click a math block or shop to use it. Driving? Just click the road ahead and your vehicle steers there. Drag to look around.

🔨 Build mode (default): left-click builds, right-click removes, drag to look. Move with the arrow keys / WASD.

Phone or tablet: drag anywhere to move (a circle appears under your thumb), tap the world to build, and tap ⭐ Use for math, JUMP to jump, Place to switch build/break.

Solve math to earn build blocks, Power Blocks, coins, or ammo — and a 🛡️ Shield from the Gloomies!

✅ Works best in Chrome — on phones, tablets, and desktop. Other browsers (like Brave) can be janky or block features; if anything looks or feels off, switch to Chrome.
